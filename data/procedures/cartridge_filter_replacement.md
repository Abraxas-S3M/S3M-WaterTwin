# Procedure: Cartridge Filter Replacement

- Document ID: PROC-CF-REPL-001
- Applies to: AST-CF-01 (5 µm cartridge filter bank, RO pretreatment)
- Type: Replacement procedure
- Status: Synthetic reference procedure for advisory use only.

## When to replace

Replace the cartridge elements when the differential pressure across the bank
reaches roughly twice the clean value (normalized dP ≈ 2.0), or on the planned
90-day interval, whichever comes first. A rising cartridge dP protects the RO
membranes from colloidal/particulate breakthrough (high SDI at the RO feed).

## Steps

1. Obtain operator approval and open a work order.
2. Divert flow to the parallel filter bank or schedule the train down.
3. Isolate and depressurize the filter housing; confirm 0 bar locally.
4. Open the housing, remove and bag spent 5 µm cartridges, and inspect for
   channeling, biofilm or premature loading.
5. Install new 5 µm cartridge set, close the housing, vent air and slowly
   re-pressurize.
6. Record the new clean differential pressure and return the bank to service.

## Spares

- Cartridge filter set (5 µm), housing O-ring kit.

> Advisory only. The platform recommends the replacement window and records the
> approval; the field action is performed by a qualified operator.
